Brayo Moha is a self-made modern man. Born and raised in Samburu County, Kenya, he is the embodiment of modern fashion professionalism. He is the CEO of Iga Ufilisike Entertainment, a model, video vixen and actor. Most of his work is inspired by his love for his home and the culture of where he is from. He has worked with a variety of artistes and professionals in the entertainment industry which are constantly featured on his Facebook and Instagram pages.

Brayo Moha is currently based in Nairobi although he has natural foothold in both Mombasa and his hometown, Maralal.
